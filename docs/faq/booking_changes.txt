Q: Can I change my KLM flight?
A: It depends on your ticket type. Basic and Light tickets cannot be changed. Standard tickets can be changed for EUR 70 plus any fare difference. Flex tickets can be changed for free, though fare difference may apply.

Q: What are the change fees for intercontinental flights?
A: Light tickets can be changed for EUR 200 plus fare difference (with some exceptions). Premium Comfort Standard tickets can be changed for EUR 250 plus fare difference.

Q: What if the new flight is cheaper?
A: If your new flight costs less, you'll receive a voucher for the remaining value rather than a cash refund.

Q: What happens to my seat selection if I change flights?
A: KLM will try to book the same seat type on your new flight. If it's not available, you can choose a different seat or receive a refund for the seat fee.

Q: What happens to my meal selection if I change flights?
A: If you reserved extra options like meals, KLM will automatically book the same ones for your new flight. If the price is higher, you pay the difference. If cheaper, you get a voucher.

Q: Can I get a refund for my seat if my flight is cancelled?
A: Yes, you're entitled to a seat refund if KLM cancels your flight, the aircraft changes making your seat unavailable, or you change flights and the same seat type isn't available.

Q: What if KLM cancels my flight?
A: You'll usually be automatically rebooked to the next available option. You can also choose a different flight through My Trip, or request a full refund if you don't wish to travel.
