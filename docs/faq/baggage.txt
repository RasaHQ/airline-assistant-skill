Q: How much checked baggage can I bring on KLM?
A: It depends on your ticket type. Basic and Light tickets have no checked baggage included. Standard or Flex tickets include 1 checked bag up to 23 kg and 158 cm total dimensions. Premium Comfort with Standard/Flex gets 2 bags. Business Class passengers get 2 bags up to 32 kg each.

Q: What are the carry-on baggage limits?
A: You can bring one carry-on bag (max 55 x 35 x 25 cm including wheels and handles) and one personal item. Carry-on must follow EU and local security regulations including liquid restrictions.

Q: How do I add extra baggage to my booking?
A: You can purchase extra baggage online up to 24 hours before departure for the lowest fees. KLM allows a maximum of 10 checked bags per passenger.

Q: What if my baggage is lost or delayed?
A: Report it immediately to the Baggage Service Office or KLM service desk at the airport. You'll receive a Property Irregularity Report (PIR) as proof of your claim.

Q: Can I bring sports equipment or musical instruments?
A: Yes, KLM has options for sports equipment, musical instruments, wedding dresses, and urns. Check specific size and weight requirements for each item type.

Q: Can I bring my pet on KLM?
A: Yes, pets can travel in the cabin or in the hold. For cabin travel, your pet and kennel together must weigh a maximum of 8 kg.

Q: How strict is KLM with baggage weight limits?
A: KLM does check baggage weight at check-in. Overweight bags may incur fees, so it's best to weigh your bags before arriving at the airport.
